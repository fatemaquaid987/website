Artsy Adventures

I am extremely fond of painting and sketching. I enjoy putting my imagination onto the canvas, sometimes with colors and sometimes without them. This page displays a few of my master pieces.

Horse

This is a detailed sketch of a horse made using pencils, charcoal, and eraser.

Still Life painting

This is a still life painting in the shades of gray, black and, white. I made it using oil paints on a 16x20 canvas.

Grisaille Self Potrait

This is a self potrait made using burnt amber oil paint on a 16x20 canvas.

Abstract Self Potrait

This is another self potrait but an abstracted one. For this piece, I used blue, yellow and, white oil colors on a 16x20 canvas.

Old Lady Potrait

This potrait of an old woman was made on a 9x12 canvas using a heavy application of acrylic paints.

Value Potrait

This is a value potrait of a young girl that I made using oil paints on a 9x12 vellum board. For this painting, I started off with black color, gradually adding white paint inorder to get the lighter value steps.
